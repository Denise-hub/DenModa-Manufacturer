Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to order?

    By clicking on the order button and filling out the form.

  • How long does it take for a customer who has ordered to receive the product?

    We give a maximum of one week.

  • How do I get the product?

    We deliver the product at your convenience. You just need to let us know the time and where to reach you.

  • Which material is used?

    Some of our products are made from yarn, which means we sew them by hand and then attach the outsole that comes from the tire. And other products are made from leather and after putting on the outsole of the tires too.

  • Do you offer good product quality service?

    Yes, our products are made from tire outsole, which makes them durable and good quality.
